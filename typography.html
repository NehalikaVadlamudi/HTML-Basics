Animals

  • Herbivorus

    • Herbivores eat only plant or plant products like sheep, cow, goat, etc.

    1. Parrot

    2. Koala

  • Omnivorus

    • Omnivores are those who eat both plants and animals, for example dogs, cats, and human beings.

    1. Hedgehog

    2. Boar

  • Carnivorus

    • Carnivores are those who can eat animals only, for example lions, tigers, etc.

    1. Tiger

    2. Lion
